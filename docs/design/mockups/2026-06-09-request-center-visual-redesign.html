Request Center — visual redesign

The structure, behavior, and the request-first IA are unchanged from what shipped — this is purely the visual layer. Three directions for the home view, same realistic content in each (grid CN87 → Washington + NE Pacific). Same header and request-basket rail across all three. Pick the direction that feels right; I'll iterate it and add the browse + GRIB views.

Cards address: icon + hierarchy (was: flat label + tiny grey text), density/spacing, layout, and location-aware emphasis (the amber-edged cards are the ones tailored to your grid).

Direction A

Briefing cards

Icon-led cards in a roomy grid. Location-tailored cards get an amber edge + a “WA / CN87” pill. Scannable, friendly, the most “designed”.
