5 · Inbound session (listener accepted a peer)

Distinct label so the operator knows the station answered — they did not dial. Abort drops the peer and re-arms. 6 · Armed but modem down (tuxlink-kw873 honesty state)

The arm record is operator intent — a modem crash must not silently flip it. The strip goes red and says the station is not hearing inbound; Re-arm retries the modem under the existing arm record, Disarm clears intent. Never again "armed in the UI, dead on the air" or the reverse.
